Leila Resedential Compound:

My first fully-opeartional serviced software project for a residential compound in New Cairo known as Leila Compound: an application that allows residents to submit complaints that are sent to the maintenance/security departments of the compound, and by which residents can view the status of their complaints, and by which the respective administrative departments can change the status of each complaint. Developed using ReactJS for a responsive frontend and Firebase for a free and reliable backend.

Duration: 1-2 Weeks
Estimated Costs by a SW company: 50-60K EGP
Actual Costs (Myself): 0 EGP
